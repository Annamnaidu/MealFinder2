Vegetarian

Vegetariansim is the parctice of abtaining from the consumption of meat (red meat, poultry, seafood, and the flesh of any of other animal), and may also include abstention from by-products if animal slaughter. Vegetarianism may be adopted for various reasons. Many people object to eating meat out of respect for sentient life. Such ethical motivations have been codified under variours religious beleifs, as well as animal rights advocacy. other motivations for vegetarianisam are health-related, political, environment, cultural, aesthetic, economic or personal perfernce. There are variations of the diet as well: an ovo-lacto vegetarian diet includes both eggs and dairy products. an ovo-vegetarian diet includes eggs but not dairy products, and a lacto-vegetarian diet includes dairy products but not eggs. A vegan diet excludes all animal products, including eggs and dairy. Some vegans also avoid other animal products such as beeswax, leather or silk clothing, and goose-fat shoe polish.
